How can I make a booking?

If you are looking to make a booking with us you can give us a call at 01743 650623. Please provide your name, the number of people in your party, and the desired date and time for your reservation.

Do you have outdoor seating?

Yes, we have outdoor seating! Our spacious outdoor area is perfect for enjoying meals in the fresh air. It’s an ideal spot for families, complete with a dedicated play area for kids, making it a delightful experience for everyone. Whether you’re here to unwind with delicious food or let the little ones have some fun, our outdoor space is designed to cater to all ages and ensure a memorable time for the whole family.

Is parking available nearby?

Yes, parking is available just outside! We have parking just outside our location, making it easy for you to visit us. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or staying longer to enjoy your time, you won’t have to worry about finding a spot.

Do you have vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free options?

Yes, we do! Our menu features a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options to ensure there's something for everyone. We're happy to accommodate dietary preferences and restrictions, so feel free to ask our staff for recommendations or customizations.

Do you offer kid-friendly meals?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated kids' menu featuring a range of delicious and nutritious options tailored to younger appetites, featuring classic favourites or smaller portions of our main dishes. It’s perfect for little ones, and combined with our outdoor play area, it ensures an enjoyable experience for the whole family.
