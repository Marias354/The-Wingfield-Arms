About the Wingfield Arms

The Wingfield Arms, a warm and historic local bar and restaurant, is located in Shropshire's lovely Shrewsbury countryside.

Located in a charming 16th-century coaching house near the old crossing of the River Severn, the pub is deeply rooted in history. The nearby Montford Bridge, a Grade II Listed structure designed by the renowned Thomas Telford, once formed part of the historic A5 road linking London to Holyhead.

Our location offers a perfect blend of convenience and tranquility. Situated adjacent to the Wingfield Caravan Park and a canoe hire business, we’re a favorite spot for campers, adventurers, and locals alike.

What We Have to Offer

The Wingfield Arms is a family-owned pub, popular with locals and visitors from neighboring villages. Our facilities include:

Bar

A lively public bar with two cozy wood-burning fireplaces, a TV, a pool table, a darts area, and even a goldfish tank.

Dining Area

A dedicated dining area and a spacious function room, ideal for gatherings or special events.

Lounge

A small, inviting lounge for quieter moments.

We serve a traditional pub menu complemented by our much-loved Sunday carvery. Our commitment to quality shines through in every dish, crafted using locally sourced meat and produce.

Family and Pet-Friendly

We pride ourselves on being a family- and pet-friendly establishment. Outside, you’ll find a beer garden, a patio area, and a children’s play area, perfect for relaxing afternoons. Fishermen and outdoor enthusiasts can also take advantage of our exclusive fishing rights along the nearby river.

Why Visit Us?

The Wingfield Arms is more than just a pub; it’s a destination. With its stunning surroundings, nearby attractions, and a delicious international menu, it’s no wonder we’ve become a favorite for both locals and travelers.
